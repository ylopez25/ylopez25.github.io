DIYPizza

"There is no Pizza like in NYC". As a New Yorker, I can agree with that quote. I have been to different states but not even Chicago Pizza beats New York City. With that being said, I wanted to build an adventure game that takes you to a pizza shop BUT ... also let's you build your own. Sounds like a dream, although sometimes it can seem a bit overwhelming when there is so many options to choose from. Luckily, in my game, it breaks it down into options. For instance, some players may be vegan, or veggitarian, and I was able to include those options as well. Furthermore, once you have selected your pizza type, it allows you to chose 3 toppings! How exciting! I was close to giving more topping availability but 3 sounded too perfect. Not too much where the pizza is heavy and messy, but definately more than two. The player gets to choose any 3 our of 5 options provided and then given an option to choose how many slices they want it cut. My mouth is watering as I think about how amazing it would be to get a bigger or smaller slice than usual. After all this fun, it is now time to look at your pizza summary and enjoy! üçï



Snowman

I'm sure you have heard of the game "Hangman". Well, Snowman is the new and improved version of it. The reason I say this is because the random word list is SUPER long which is amazing. If you are a competitve player like I am, then I can assure you that you will be playing Snowman for a while...unless you really are good. Not only is this game super fun and challenging, but it also gives you different number of chances everytime. The longer the word, the more chances you have, but you better hope they are the correct letters guessed for it to count. In addition to different number of chances, you are not limited to the amount of times you can guess a new word. So you can never say you are bored!
